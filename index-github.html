Wave Goodbye to your old CMS, say hello to Pingendo.

Pingendo for Github let you, your team and clients collaborate on Github-workflow to build static websites, giving everyone early and frequent opportunities to view, approve and change the html pages being delivered.

An alternative to most CMS that are often clunky, overcomplicated and customers may not see what will be delivered until the site is almost finished. By that time, changes can be difficult (and costly) to implement.
