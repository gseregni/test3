Efficient collaboration between developers and non developers for a great communication.

Developers

Developers have the privilege to iterate on a real page showing in the browser. 
Pingendo extend this feature to non-devs.
Thus, they become more independent and take care of wording themselves, relying on dev for coding only.

Non developers

Clients, stakeholders, designers, copywriters and marketers are by far those who iterate most on a website.
Pingendo takes them in the browser, in front of the real page, iterating side by side with developers.
A great bite to back and forth of emails, docs, psd, mockups and other offline tools. 

Together

Pingendo is for teams with at least one dev involved. 
Dev codes and manages everyone and everything on Github, the most adopted revision control system.
Non developers will iterate on webpages, each one according to its role and skill.

Two dedicated tools to keep everyone in the loop.
