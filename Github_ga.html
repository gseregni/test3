Efficient collaboration between developers and non-developers for a great communication

Developers

The whole team iterates in the browser, on real pages, since first draft and generating clean HTML/CSS on Github.
A real help for dev to leave minor tasks to content makers and focus on coding requests, clearly submitted as pull requests.

Non developers

Clients, stakeholders, designers, copywriters and marketers are by far those who work most on a website creation.
Pingendo takes them in the browser, iterating in front of the real page, side by side with developers.
Will be less needed time-consuming actions back and forth of emails, docs, psd, mockups, presentations or approval meeting.                                          

Together

Pingendo is for teams with at least one developer involved. 
Dev codes and manages everyone and everything on Github, the most adopted revision control system.
Non developers freely and safely iterate on webpages, each one according to its own role and skill.
