Are there any trial?

The free plan is full feature.

How do I subscribe to repos?

From the account page check/uncheck the repos you want to activate/deactivate. Deactivation of a repo keep it working till the end of the paid period.

What about payments?

Payments are managed by Fastspring that accepts all major credit cards and Paypal.

How billing works?

Billing is per repo x month.  Each one is billed separately accordingly to its activation date. 

Can I cancel any time?

Yes, just deactivate all repos from your account page.
