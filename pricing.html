Are there any trial?

Go with the free plan, it's full features out of private repos.

How do I subscribe to private repos?

From the account page you can activate/deactivate each repository on the Github account you are signed in. Deactivating a repository keeps it working till the end of the paid period.

What about payments?

Payments are managed by Fastspring that accepts all major credit cards and Paypal.

How billing works?

Billing is per repository x month.  Each repository is billed separately accordingly to its activation date. 

Can I cancel any time?

Yes, just deactivate all repositories from your account page. They will keep working till the end of the paid period.
