the concept we want to transmit is that with Pingendo you work on "standard" "vanilla" "real" html and sass files on your disk. So you can use them with external editors, git client , Grunt etc.
Similar product like Webflow, blocsapp etc instead use proprietary and closed and non standard solutions to save their files. So they cannot access the 1000x features available in the for standard HTML/CSS.
